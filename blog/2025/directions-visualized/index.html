Oct 10, 2025

Over the past weekend, I worked on uplifting the Areopa Website to a new, more modern, slick view. I used the opportunity to give the mindmap visualization of all Areopa webinars a new home on the main website. Check the site out, let me know what you think about the new design!

Before: Original Areopa design

After: New modern design

As I was going through with it, I was thinking if I could now visualize something else, so I can keep a different mindmap on the blog. And with Directions EMEA just around the corner, with the record number of sessions, I found my perfect candidate!

It’s never easy to find the sessions you really want to attend, and with over 240 available ones, it’s even harder this year. Are you an AI fan? Or a sceptic? Well, this view will help you find exactly the sessions you are interested in. When the agenda is public, I’ll adjust the entries with the dates and times of sessions.
